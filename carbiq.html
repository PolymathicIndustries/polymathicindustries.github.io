CarbIQ makes it easy to track your carbs wherever you are. Whether you're at home, eating with friends, or shopping for groceries, you can log meals quickly and stay on top of your daily carb intake without guesswork.

The app is designed for speed and simplicity, helping you record what you eat and understand how it fits into your daily goals. No complicated setup or calculations—just clear, accurate tracking when you need it.

CarbIQ also includes a built-in food label analyzer, allowing you to scan and review nutrition labels so you can make informed decisions before buying or eating. This makes it easier to stick to your routine, even when trying new foods.

It’s built for people following keto, low-carb, Atkins, or diabetes-focused diets, helping reduce the stress of tracking and making consistency easier over time.

You can also explore unique in-app items, including limited-time offers that rotate regularly.

Download CarbIQ today and simplify your carb tracking.
