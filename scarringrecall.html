Game · Scarring Recall

A narrative mystery about the memories you avoid.

Two brothers, a fractured childhood, and the emotional weight that was never spoken aloud.

Scarring Recall invites players into the space between memory, fear, and loyalty — and asks what it takes to truly understand someone you thought you knew.

Scarring Recall is a narrative mystery game about a man returning to the memories he has avoided for most of his life. The story follows Ryan as he searches for the truth behind a fractured childhood and the distance that grew between him and his brother. What begins as a simple attempt to understand the past becomes a journey through the emotional weight they both carried but never spoke about.

Players explore key moments, uncover clues, and move through a series of intimate confrontations that reveal how misunderstandings, silence, and trauma shaped the brothers in different ways. Each encounter focuses on connection, perspective, and the difficulty of facing the people who mattered most when things went wrong.

Scarring Recall is not only a mystery to solve. It is a story about two brothers trying to understand each other when communication has failed. The game invites players to step into the emotional space between them and witness how memory, fear, and loyalty collide as Ryan pieces together what truly happened.
