CarbIQ is a lightweight tool designed to help people build a natural sense for carbohydrate amounts without the burden of tracking. Instead of logging meals or counting macros, CarbIQ uses repetition and intuition-building to help users develop a reliable internal gauge.

Each calculation becomes a small learning moment. Over time, these moments add up, creating a quiet but powerful shift in awareness. CarbIQ is not about precision — it’s about instinct.
